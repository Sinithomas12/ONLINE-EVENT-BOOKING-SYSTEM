Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about the event, venue, registration, and more.

How do I register for the event?

You can register online by clicking the "Register" button on our homepage. Early registration is recommended as seats are limited.

What is included in the ticket price?

Your ticket includes full access to all sessions, keynote talks, workshops, lunch, coffee breaks, and post-event downloadable content.

Can I get a refund if I can’t attend?

Yes, we offer full refunds up to 14 days before the event. After that, tickets are non-refundable, but you can transfer

Are there discounts for groups or students?

Yes, we offer group discounts for 5 or more attendees and special rates for students with valid ID. Contact our support team for discount codes.

Will session recordings be available after the event?

Yes. All registered participants will receive access to video recordings and presentation slides within a week after the event.

What health and safety measures are in place?

We follow local health guidelines to ensure safety. Hand sanitizing stations, optional masks, and contactless check-in will be available at the venue.
